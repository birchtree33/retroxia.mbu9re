MODIFIED RESPAWNABLE BOMBING UNIT, AND PLANS FOR MASS PRODUCTION.

MBU-9re, otherwise known as Modified Bombing Unit-9 Respawnable is a humanoid volatile construct whose primary goal is to cause chaos. A machine forged for terrorism and destruction. It is built to look like a robloxian unlike other machines such as the killbots or beacons. This allows it to access more limited areas and giving it more mobility.

Like all BUs, it has exceptional speed thanks to its specialized engines. Its armor is made mostly out of bluesteel with some blackiron to reduce weight and allow for faster acceleration. Unlike other bombing units however, it contains trace amounts of viridis to allow for easier repairability. Small pieces of the armor were tested and attuned with against a variety of lua energy, with the armor notably being more resistant to brandcast but faces far weaker efficiency from any healing that is casted from lua.

By inserting LUA into an artificial spawnheart, it allowed for the mechanical being to regenerate and repair itself slowly which pairs well with the trace amounts of viridis, unlike other bombing units. This also allows it to respawn on spawn points. It is due to this that we have dipped in reputation with the Order of Spawn as it is unnatural for machines to have a spawnheart, let alone one created from artificial means. The unit is equipped with a core attuned with brandcast lua that will soon contain a soul of a robloxian in order to give what most people would call "traits" to it.

Plans of adding a mind into MBU-9re has also been talked about however we aren't completely sure if this is possible as there has been no counts of successful mind transfer from one being to another, let alone an artificial being. Some argue that the procedure of transferring a robloxian's mind to an artificial being is simply too costly and overall not beneficial. Thus as for now, MBU-9re will completely rely on it's advanced machine-learning programming that while more limited than having a mind, is enough to suffice allowing the unit to remember past encounters. Furthermore, using programming provides the benefit of the machine being able to learn from digital sources such as disks, hard-drives, and other data containers, physical or not.

However, there have been some notable vulnerabilities spotted in the making of this specialized bombing unit, particularly involving acid. Acid works very well at hindering MBU-9re's natural regeneration whilst weakening and chipping away at it's armour. Poiseswirl also appears to be rather affective at melting away the external blackiron-bluesteel alloy armour. Another vulnerability involves the programming being prone to being interrupted by lua and malware which may cause the unit to slow down in processing speeds.

As mentioned earlier, this will heavily tarnish our current reputation with the Order of Spawn and may even lead to cooperation efforts and contracts being cancelled or revoked. We fear that if we lose such an ally it may lead to ████████████ Corporation facing heavy losses both in profit and manpower. Thus this document must be kept top secret to avoid any mishaps with the order for as long as we can muster.
