CONTAINMENT INFORMATION:

MBU-9re is to be contained within a 90 by 90 stud area with 50 stud high walls that are 5 studs thick and made of linkiron with trace amounts of adurite. The observation window has two layers with a 0.5 stud gap inbetween. The bulletproof outer layer facing the containment chamber is reinforced with blueesteel. The inner layer on the side of the observation room is regular bulletproof glass. Both layers are slightly attuned with Spiritphase and Lightrevel to create a one-way view without weakening the glass. Two security cameras are to watch down at MBU-9re and are constantly operated between shifts. There are various machinery in the observation room, with three panels controlling the cameras; one for broadcasting, lights and security alert; and one for tracking and logging the subject's current status in a database. Three researchers are required to operate the observation room for every shift, should one disappear, another will backup their place. Two security guards are to be stationed outside the door of the containment chamber. Should MBU-9re escape, the facility will alert all personnel to stay on guard and have security move immediately to any choke-points or exits to intercept the subject.
